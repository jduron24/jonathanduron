## Overview

SignalRank is a job market intelligence platform built to analyze large volumes of job descriptions and extract meaningful patterns about hiring trends, skill demand, and role expectations across industries.

Instead of treating job postings as isolated listings, the platform aggregates and normalizes data from thousands of roles to identify what companies are actually looking for. By applying natural language processing and structured analysis, SignalRank transforms unstructured job descriptions into a unified dataset that reveals how skills cluster together, how requirements differ by company type, and how expectations evolve over time.

The system helps surface insights such as which technologies are most in demand, what combinations of skills lead to higher-paying roles, and how job titles translate into real-world responsibilities. By converting raw listings into structured intelligence, SignalRank provides a clearer view of the labor market for both individuals and organizations.

## What I worked on

- Built scalable web scraping pipelines to collect job descriptions from multiple sources and aggregate them into a centralized dataset.
- Designed normalization processes to standardize job titles, skills, and responsibilities across inconsistent listing formats.
- Implemented natural language processing techniques to extract key entities such as technologies, experience requirements, and role expectations.
- Developed clustering and pattern analysis to identify relationships between skills, tools, and job categories.
- Created trend detection systems to track rising and declining technologies across time.
- Built backend services using FastAPI to process, analyze, and serve structured job market data.
- Designed analytics layers to compare roles across startups, mid-size companies, and large enterprises.
- Generated insight-driven outputs such as skill gap analysis, role benchmarking, and market demand summaries.
- Structured datasets to support downstream applications like recommendation engines and resume optimization tools.
- Focused on transforming raw data into actionable intelligence rather than static dashboards.

## Outcome

SignalRank turns fragmented job postings into a cohesive view of the job market, helping uncover what skills actually matter and how hiring expectations are changing.

By analyzing patterns across thousands of listings, the platform provides clarity on in-demand technologies, role definitions, and competitive skill combinations. It reduces guesswork for individuals navigating their careers and creates a foundation for data-driven decision-making in hiring, recruiting, and workforce planning.

Instead of relying on anecdotal advice or isolated job posts, SignalRank enables a more systematic understanding of the labor market, making it easier to align skills, opportunities, and strategy with real-world demand.
